Data Security and Privacy

The move to a standardized and more centralized EHR creates personal data security concerns. The larger scale of a centralized EHR system increases the potential size and impact of a data breach. The sensitivity of this data aids in intensifying the impact of any personal information being leaked, as this highly detailed and private data is used to target vulnerable people for blackmail or identity theft 1.
